Naruto Shippuden

Naruto: Shippūden (NARUTO -ナルト- 疾風伝, Naruto Shippūden)

iterally meaning: Naruto Hurricane Chronicles is the anime adaptation of Part II of the Naruto manga, set two-and-a-half years after Naruto Uzumaki leaves Konohagakure to train with Jiraiya. The series had 500 episodes, which originally aired in Japan on TV Tokyo from 2007 until 2017.
